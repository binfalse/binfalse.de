--- layout: page title: mediadump --- Script to dump streams of different mediathek's. You can call it with various `.asx` files provided by for example the mediathek of ZDF or 3sat and it will dump the stream to the `.asx` 's directory. All you need is the mplayer. Download it directly from your terminal: {% highlight bash %} wget /wp-content/uploads/pipapo/scripts/mediadump {% endhighlight %}
Download: Bash: mediadump (Please take a look at the man-page. Browse bugs and feature requests.)
