--- layout: page title: 'SquirrelMail search reorder' --- Resort the search results of SquirrelMail. The search results of SquirrelMail are ordered from the oldest to the newest mail. But you probably often search for the newest mail, and so every time you perform a search you have to scroll to the bottom of the page... This userscript resorts all mails, so that the newest mail is on the top of the page! A description of the functionality you can find in the corresponding blog article.
